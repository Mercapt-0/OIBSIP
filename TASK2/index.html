ARIJIT

SINGH

SINGER

Arijit Singh is a renowned Indian playback singer known for his soulful and melodious voice. Born on April 25, 1987, in West Bengal, India, he rose to fame in the Bollywood music industry and quickly became one of its most sought-after vocalists.

His breakthrough came with the song "Tum Hi Ho" from the film "Aashiqui 2," which catapulted him to stardom. Arijit Singh is celebrated for his ability to convey deep emotions through his singing and has won several awards for his remarkable contributions to Indian music.

Arijit's singing talent and versatility have made him a household name, with numerous chart-topping hits to his credit. He has a vast discography that spans romantic ballads, peppy tracks, and soul-stirring melodies, making him a beloved figure in the world of Indian music.
